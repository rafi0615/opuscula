Abstract:

We consider the robust chromatic number \(\chi_1(G)\) of planar graphs \(G\) and show that there exists an infinite family of planar graphs \(G\) with \(\chi_1(G) = 3\), thus solving a recent problem of Bacs√≥ et al. from [The robust chromatic number of graphs, Graphs Combin. 40 (2024), #89].
