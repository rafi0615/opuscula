Abstract:

In this paper new monotonic properties of nonoscillatory solutions for second order linear functional differential equations with delayed argument $$ y''(t) = p(t)y(œÑ(t)) $$ have been established. New properties are used to introduce criteria for elimination of bounded nonoscillatory solutions for studied equations.
