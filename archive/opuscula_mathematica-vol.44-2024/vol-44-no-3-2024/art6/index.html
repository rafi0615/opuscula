Abstract:

In this semi-expository short note, we prove that the only homogeneous pure hyponormal operator \(T\) with \(\operatorname{\mathbf{rank}}(T^{*}T - TT^{*}) = 1\), modulo unitary equivalence, is the unilateral shift.

[hyponormal operator, multiplicity, trace formula, homogeneous operators, principal function]
